About us

We leading provider of innovative elevator solutions for commercial and residential buildings.

Who we are?

Saya elevator is a leading supplier of elevator machines & spare parts. We are totally focused on fulfillment of customers needs. Saya's policy is to manufacture & deliver timely elevator machines, that will perform reliably & effectively in total fulfillment of customers requirement.

Our elevator lift machine have been designed for use by the lift industry.Our below traction machine conform to highest safety & performance standard, & thus are the natural choice of leading national & international elevator manufacture.We are adequately supported by our vendors, who ensure quality & timely supplies. We take great pride in our relationships with our vendors, who play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and timely delivery of our products. With their support, we are able to provide our customers with the exceptional service and products that they deserve. Whether you need a new elevator machine or spare parts for your existing equipment, Saya Enterprises is the name to trust. Contact us today to learn more about our products and services, and experience the difference that our commitment to customer satisfaction can make.

What We Offer?

With competitive pricing and exceptional customer service, we are committed to meeting all of your elevator part needs.

Diverse range of parts

We offer a comprehensive range of elevator parts, including but not limited to, motors, control systems, door operators, cables, sensors, switches, and more.

Quality and reliability

All of our elevator parts are manufactured to the highest industry standards, ensuring the highest levels of quality and reliability.

Expert guidance

Our team of experts has years of experience in the elevator industry, providing guidance and support to ensure your elevator operates at peak performance.

Competitive pricing

we understand that keeping costs low is important without compromising on quality. That's why we offer competitive pricing on all of our elevator parts, ensuring that you get the best value for your money.
